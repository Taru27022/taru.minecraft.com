Essential Shortcuts

F3 Debug Screen - View coordinates, biome info, and performance data
F5 Camera View - Switch between first and third person perspectives
Shift Sneak - Move quietly and prevent falling off edges
Ctrl Sprint - Run faster and jump further distances
Space Jump - Leap over obstacles and climb blocks
Q Drop Item - Quickly drop selected items from inventory
E Inventory - Open your inventory and crafting interface
F11 Fullscreen - Toggle fullscreen mode for immersive gameplay
1-9 Hotbar - Quickly select items in your hotbar slots
Tab Player List - View online players in multiplayer mode
T Chat - Open chat window to communicate with other players
F2 Screenshot - Capture and save screenshots of your world
